# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Query In to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming open-source project. Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate with care, assume good intent, and keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give clear technical feedback without personal attacks.
- Welcome newcomers and explain decisions when context is useful.
- Keep issue and pull request discussions on topic.
- Respect privacy and do not share private data, credentials, or user files.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal insults or sustained hostile conduct.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent loss of participation privileges in the project.

If you need to report a concern, open a private security advisory or contact the repository owner through GitHub.
